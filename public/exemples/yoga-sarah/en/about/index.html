Sarah Mitchell — from London to Strasbourg, from marketing to yoga

500h certified yoga teacher (Himalayan Yoga Academy, Rishikesh, 2022), based in Strasbourg since 2023. Sarah teaches Hatha, Vinyasa and Yin Yoga in French and English at Studio La Parenthèse.

Born in London in 1984, Sarah spent 12 years in digital marketing — at Ogilvy, Wavemaker, then as account director at Fold7 in Shoreditch.

"I was good at my job. That was the problem — I had no reason to stop."

The turning point

In 2020, burnout ended that career. It was in her London flat, on a mat borrowed from her flatmate, that Sarah discovered yoga. In 2022, she invested her savings in a 500h YTT in Rishikesh, at the foot of the Himalayas. Five months of intensive practice.

"My mum thought I'd joined a cult. My ex-colleagues thought I was having a midlife crisis at 38."

Strasbourg

Sarah followed her partner Julien to Alsace in 2023. First class at Studio La Parenthèse: 3 students. A post in the "Strasbourg Expats Community" group changed everything — 14 sign-ups in one week. Today: 8 weekly slots and over 50 regular students.

"Yoga is not about touching your toes, it's about what you learn on the way down."

Certifications

  • 500h YTT — Himalayan Yoga Academy, Rishikesh (2022)
  • 200h Yin Yoga — Insight Yoga Institute, Sarah Powers method (2023)
  • MBSR 8 weeks — mindfulness (2021)
