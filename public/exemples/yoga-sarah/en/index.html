Three disciplines, one approach: yoga without the fluff

From traditional Hatha to dynamic Vinyasa, through Yin Yoga and meditation — every class is taught with rigour, kindness, and a touch of humour.

Hatha Yoga

The foundation of yoga. Held postures, guided breath, precise alignment. Perfect for beginners or deepening your practice. Sarah teaches classical Hatha as she learned it in Rishikesh — no ambient music, no scented candles, just you and your mat.

Vinyasa Flow

A fluid sequence of postures synchronised with breath. Dynamic but accessible — Sarah adapts each session so beginners and experienced practitioners progress together. Curated playlist included.

Yin Yoga & Meditation

Passive postures held for 3 to 5 minutes, working on meridians and letting go. Guided meditation completes the session — mindfulness, visualisation, or simply learning to do nothing for 45 minutes. Harder than it sounds.

Sarah's journey

500h certified yoga teacher (Himalayan Yoga Academy, Rishikesh, 2022), based in Strasbourg since 2023. Sarah teaches Hatha, Vinyasa and Yin Yoga in French and English at Studio La Parenthèse.

Born in London in 1984, Sarah spent 12 years in digital marketing — at Ogilvy, Wavemaker, then as account director at Fold7 in Shoreditch.

"I was good at my job. That was the problem — I had no reason to stop."
