Yoga retreats in the Vosges — immersive weekends

Two days of yoga, meditation and hiking at the Gîte du Hohwald, in the Alsatian mountains. Vegetarian meals, accommodation included, groups of 12 maximum.

Sarah's retreats are Rishikesh without the plane ticket. A full weekend in the Vosges mountains: sunrise yoga facing the peaks, forest hikes, guided meditation, pranayama workshops, vegetarian meals prepared together. All in a mountain lodge in Le Hohwald, 50 minutes from Strasbourg. No need to fly to Bali — Alsace has everything you need.

What's included

  • 4 yoga sessions (Hatha, Vinyasa, Yin)
  • 2 guided meditations (sunrise + evening)
  • 1 guided hike in the Vosges
  • 1 pranayama workshop (breathing techniques)
  • Shared room accommodation (2-3 people)
  • Vegetarian meals (Saturday lunch → Sunday lunch)
  • Herbal teas and snacks throughout

€280 all-inclusive

(accommodation + meals + all activities)

The venue: Gîte du Hohwald

The Gîte du Hohwald is a charming wood-and-stone lodge nestled at 800 metres altitude in the Alsatian Vosges. Terrace with valley views, bright practice room, garden.
