Frequently asked questions about yoga classes

I've never done yoga before — can I come?

Absolutely. Most of Sarah's students started with zero experience. Every posture is offered with variations adapted to all levels. As Sarah says: "Whether you can touch your toes or not, you're welcome."

Which type of yoga is best for beginners?

Hatha Yoga is ideal to start — held postures, calm pace, focus on fundamentals. Monday morning (9 AM) and Wednesday morning (10 AM) classes are particularly suited for beginners.

Are the English classes also in French?

Classes marked "EN" are primarily in English, with French reformulations if needed. "FR/EN" classes are taught in both languages simultaneously. You'll understand everything, whatever your language.

How do I book a class?

No online booking needed for group classes — just show up 10 minutes before the class at Studio La Parenthèse, 7 rue des Dentelles. For private sessions, workshops and retreats, contact Sarah via the form or by phone.

Do I need to bring my own mat?

The studio has mats, but if you have your own, bring it — it's always more comfortable. Blocks, straps and meditation cushions are provided.

What are the prices?

Drop-in class at €15, or 10-class card at €120 (€12/class), 20-class card at €200 (€10/class), unlimited monthly at €85. Private session: €60. Themed workshop: €35.

Do you offer corporate yoga?

Yes. Sarah comes to your office for 45-minute sessions, in French or English. €120 per session for up to 15 people. Perfect for lunch breaks or after work.

How does a retreat work?

Full weekend from Saturday morning to Sunday afternoon at the Gîte du Hohwald. 4 yoga sessions, 2 meditations, 1 hike, vegetarian meals included. €280 all-inclusive. Groups of 12 maximum.

Ready to unroll your mat?

Whether you're a beginner or experienced practitioner, there's a class for you. No booking needed for group classes — just come as you are.
